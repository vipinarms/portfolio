Project Nexus

Timeline6 Months

The Challenge

The Delivery Excellence team manages the quality and health of every project in the company. Their legacy workflow relied entirely on fragmented **Excel files**, leading to manual data entry errors, missed audit deadlines, and a lack of real-time visibility into project risks.

Legacy Friction

  • ⚠️ Manual Email "Nudges" for Feedback
  • ⚠️ Static Audit Planners in Excel
  • ⚠️ Reactive Risk Management
  • ⚠️ Zero Cross-Project Visibility

Project Nexus Solution

  • ✅ Automated CEM Reminder Engine
  • ✅ Dynamic Audit & Resource Planner
  • ✅ Predictive Risk Dashboards
  • ✅ Unified Training & Schedule Management

The Digital Ecosystem

We didn't just build a database; we built a workflow engine that houses 6 mission-critical modules under one interface.

CEM Management

Digitized Customer Experience forms with an automated reminder system that tracks "Client Response Rates."

Audit Planner

A smart calendar view for audit management that automatically schedules checks based on project complexity.

Resource & Training

Strategic modules to track team skill-gaps and allocate resources based on live project demands.

Risk Dashboard

A proactive risk register that flags projects in "Red" or "Amber" status before they impact delivery.

Design Deep-Dive

As the Lead Designer, I focused on **Information Architecture (IA)**. The biggest hurdle was fitting Audit, Risk, Schedule, and Training into a sidebar without overwhelming the user.

"The new interface prioritizes 'Action Items'—users no longer hunt for data; the app tells them what needs attention today."
