GEAR UP NC VR

Increasing college access for first-generation students through immersive 360-degree virtual reality experiences.

PlatformiOS & Android
ClientUNC System
DurationMobile Project
Tech360 VR Video

The Project Goal

GEAR UP NC (Gaining Early Awareness and Readiness for Undergraduate Programs) is a federally funded program intended to help students, particularly first-generation college-goers, visualize their future in higher education.

Key Objective

To provide an early, immersive look at the 16 University of North Carolina campuses, motivating students to apply for college and financial aid.

"The project aims to increase the number of students prepared to enter and succeed in postsecondary education".

App Ecosystem

Introductory 360 Video

A high-immersion welcome video on the home page introducing users to the GEAR UP NC VR world.

Campus Life & Discovery

360-degree tours covering general information about NC colleges, campus life, and specific majors/degrees.

Visit NC Colleges

A comprehensive directory of all UNC-system universities, each featuring overview videos and student life content.

Direct Connection

Direct integration with admissions via email and links to financial aid, degree information, and social media.

The VR Experience

The app is optimized for Google Cardboard. Users can toggle "Cardboard Mode" in the video player to transform their smartphone into a VR headset.

  • • Interactive QR Code scanning for instant setup.
  • • Seamless switching between 360-view and VR-mode.
  • • Contextual video transcripts for accessibility.

Coming Soon

AI integration for admissions bots, allowing students to text questions directly to the university through the app.

Impact & Reach

Starting as a pilot with the UNC Chapel Hill Visitor Center, the project now encompasses all 16 UNC campuses, with plans to expand into North Carolina Community Colleges.
